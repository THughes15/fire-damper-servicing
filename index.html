BS:9999 – Annual Servicing Is a Legal Requirement

British Standard BS:9999 sets out the fire safety code of practice for building design, management and use. It clearly states that fire dampers and their control systems must be inspected, tested and serviced at least once every 12 months.

Failure to comply can lead to enforcement action under the Regulatory Reform (Fire Safety) Order 2005, invalidate insurance policies and expose duty‑holders to prosecution. FireSafe Systems delivers fully‑documented annual servicing so you can demonstrate compliance and protect lives, property and your legal position.
